The Reset

The Reset: Trauma, Treatment Resistance, and How Psychedelics Could Save America is a timely and critically important work by Melina Bricker. This book offers original research, compelling narratives, and modern best practices to address the crisis of trauma and poor health in America. In an effort to bring forth the overwhelming evidence of psychedelic-assisted therapy efficacy in behavioral health, trauma researcher Melina Bricker highlights rampant trauma-related treatment resistance as a rallying cry for critical thought and widespread legalization of plant-based medicines in the United States. Including original phenomenological research, compelling lived history, and special advocacy for Veterans, The Reset offers something traditional corporate therapies and limited treatment options cannot: hope.
